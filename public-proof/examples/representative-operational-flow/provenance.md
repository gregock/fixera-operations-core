# Provenance

This sample is derived from the private `fixera-core` operational model, but it is not a direct export from production.

## Source basis

- The public architecture notes in this portfolio
- The documented job lifecycle and decision-support model
- The private system's general pattern of intake, validation, state tracking, and reporting

## Transformation rule

- Reduce one representative service job into a synthetic public example.
- Keep only the field shapes and state transitions needed to explain the system.
- Replace all real values with invented values that preserve the structure.

## Redaction rule

- Remove any live client data.
- Remove any reference to a real business event.
- Remove private context, operational history, and runtime details.

## Verification rule

- Confirm the sample matches the public architecture and remains obviously synthetic.
- Confirm the sample cannot be mistaken for a production export.
